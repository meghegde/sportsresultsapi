Hello, and welcome to the Sports Results API!

You can easily get match scores for your favourite league by visiting the following endpoint: '/api/v1/scores/:league'.

Available leagues are:

  • 'NBA' - National Basketball Association, Basketball, USA
  • 'PL' - Premier League, Football, UK
Simply visit the relevant page of the scores API e.g. '/api/v1/scores/PL' to get all the match scores for the season.
